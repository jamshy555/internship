Laptop

A laptop is a portable computer designed for everyday use, combining performance and convenience in one device. It is widely used for studying, office work, programming, and entertainment. Modern laptops come with powerful processors, large storage, and high-quality displays, making them suitable for multitasking and creative tasks. They also offer features like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and long battery life, allowing users to work from anywhere. Popular brands include Dell, HP, Lenovo, and Apple, each offering different models for various needs and budgets.
